The Abbey Wall

A record of those who walked through, and what the circus remembers.

The tent is quieter now. The candles are burning low, the organ has stopped, and the fortune teller has closed her eyes — though whether she’s sleeping or simply done with you is anyone’s guess.

Your creature is yours. Your title is earned. The things you found inside these rooms — the choices you made, the stories you told, the objects that chose you back — those are written now, in ink the circus doesn’t let fade.

This was always for you. All of it. Every room, every thread of music, every impossible thing on every impossible shelf. Built by two people who wanted to give you something worth remembering on a night worth celebrating.

Now go. The celebrations are waiting on Discord, the drinks are poured, and if you don’t show up soon, someone will send the raccoon apothecary after you.

And trust us — it has opinions.

With love, mischief, and an unreasonable amount of code — Kay & Dax
